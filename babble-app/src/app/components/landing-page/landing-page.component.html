About Us

We are a young team of enthusiasts working towards achieving a common goal of making the process of speaking english effective and fun.

Envisioned during the COVID pandemic, a time when the world came to a standstill, our purpose was is to help people make the best use of their time by honing the indispensable skill of speaking english in a fluent and confident manner, in the comfort of their homes.

Numerous courses advertise the promise to enhance your English proficieny, but offer the same old techniques.

Our team seeks to tackle the problem from a fresh perspective, by providing a safe learning space and focusing purely on guided PRACTICE.
